Private Policy

MealMates is committed to protecting the privacy of our donors, volunteers, and beneficiaries in accordance with the Australian Privacy Principles under the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth). We collect personal information such as names, contact details, and donation history solely for the purpose of facilitating our operations and improving our services. This information is kept confidential and is not shared with any third parties except as required by law. We employ appropriate security measures to ensure that your data is protected against unauthorised access, alteration, or disclosure.

By engaging with MealMates, you consent to the collection and use of your information in accordance with this policy. If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy practices, or if you wish to update or remove your information from our records, please contact us at realmealmates@outlook.com. We are dedicated to maintaining the trust and confidence of our community and regularly review and update our privacy practices to ensure compliance with current regulations and best practices.

We also gather non-personal data, such as feedback, service usage statistics, and anonymized demographic information to better understand the needs and preferences of our community. This data helps us to enhance our programs, improve user experiences, and measure the impact of our services. Rest assured that any such data is used in aggregate form, ensuring that individual identities remain confidential. MealMates is committed to transparency and integrity in all aspects of data management, and we regularly audit our data collection and handling processes to ensure they align with ethical standards and legal requirements. Your trust is paramount, and we strive to create a secure and respectful environment for all data interactions.
