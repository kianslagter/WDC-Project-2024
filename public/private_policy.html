Private Policy

MealMates is committed to protecting the privacy of our donors, volunteers, and beneficiaries in accordance with the Australian Privacy Principles under the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth). We are dedicated to maintaining the trust and confidence of our community and regularly review and update our privacy practices to ensure compliance with current regulations and best practices.
